About Me

Hi there! I'm Marissa and I'm excited to share a bit about myself. I have a pretty diverse background. I majored in Cultural Studies, Literature, and Linguistics, which gives me a unique way of looking at things. I've gone from crafting delicious pastries as a Pastry Chef to diving into the tech world as a Full Stack Developer Apprentice.

I'm really excited about the potential to make a big impact in financial planning and supply chain management using Anaplan. I'm eager to explore innovative applications that can streamline these processes and bring about some real change.

When it comes to web development, I'm all about enhancing user experiences with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Bootstrap 5. I also love automating tasks with tools like Selenium to extract and use data in cool ways.

My portfolio is a mix of creative and technical projects, showing my commitment to learning and growing continuously. When I'm not working, you'll often find me on my yoga mat, at rock concert, or just enjoying some great tunes. I'm all about inspiration, constant movement, and balance.

Thanks for stopping by my portfolio! I'd love to connect and see how we can create something amazing together.
